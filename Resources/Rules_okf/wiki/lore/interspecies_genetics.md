# Overview
Founded by the legendary Doctor Ptolus during the Golden Age, this project aimed to create viable offspring between distinct galactic races.

## The Rhone Connection
The project discovered that the genetic structure of Rhones had a peculiar relationship with almost all other races. By using Rhonish genes as a bridge, the project produced bipedal hybrids capable of breathing nitrogen/oxygen atmospheres.

## Consequences
These hybrids were often rejected by their parent races and restricted to relocation camps. This tension eventually fueled the Galactic Extermination Wars.
