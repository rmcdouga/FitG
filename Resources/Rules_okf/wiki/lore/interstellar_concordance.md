# Overview
The Interstellar Concordance represents a "Golden Age" where member planets lived in democratic harmony and galactic commerce flourished.

## The Segundens Discovery
The era was sparked by the discovery of instantaneous interstellar communication, which ended planetary isolation and created a galactic stock exchange.

## Collapse
The Concordance fell during the Galactic Extermination Wars, as member planets destroyed everything of value in a fanatical drive for local domination. By the end, the achievements of this era became mysterious relics.
