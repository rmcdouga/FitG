# Overview
Janis the Lucky was a physicist and colonist who founded the Empire after discovering the secrets of an ancient FTL drive.

## The Discovery
Janis found the "Idol of Unarpha," a self-contained faster-than-light drive left behind by the defunct Interstellar Concordance. This discovery allowed him to scatter seeds of Imperial pollination across the stars, establishing his dominance.

## Legacy
While Janis was not inherently evil, his belief in his own destiny and the power of the Idol led to the creation of a regime that evolved into an oppressive force under terms of succession.
