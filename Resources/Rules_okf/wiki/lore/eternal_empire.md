# Overview
The Eternal Empire is a vast regime that has held the galaxy in its grasp for centuries. Its power is based on the centralization of authority and the control of faster-than-light (FTL) drives.

## Order of Governance
The Empire's rule is established through a system of provinces, with the "First Province" serving as the political power base. The center of the empire is the Throne Planet, Orlog.

## The Imperial Knights
The Order of the Imperial Knights, established centrally under Redjac, serves to protect the Emperor and preserve the Empire's stability, often through fear and strategic force.
