# Overview
Information android's circuits overload; attempts to blow any one character's mind.

## Combat Stats
- **Intelligence**: 3
- **Endurance**: 3

## Special Rules
- **Mental Battle**: Utilizes Intelligence ratings, not strength.
- **Damage**: Intelligence ratings are not affected by reduction of endurance.
- **Victory**: Characters must succeed in breaking off to end the attack.
