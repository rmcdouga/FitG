# Overview
Intelligent trees that are quick to anger when stirred.

## Combat Stats
- **Base Strength**: 5
- **Base Endurance**: 5

## Special Rules
- **Growth**: Add one to strength and endurance *each* round of combat (except the first). This addition is cumulative.
- **Victory Condition**: If endurance is reduced to 0, the combat ends with a character victory.
