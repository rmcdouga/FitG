# Overview
Members of a religious cult that worships and develops the powers of the mind.

## Combat Stats
- **Intelligence**: 5
- **Endurance**: 5
- **Defense**: Characters use their combined Intelligence ratings to defend.

## Special Rules
- **Mental Attack**: Combat results reduce endurance as per the rules, but character intelligence (and Drusers') is not reduced.
- **Break-off**: Determined by comparing Intelligence ratings.
