# Overview
Gelatinous blob that grows stronger as it is dealt blasts and blows.

## Combat Stats
- **Base Strength**: 3
- **Endurance**: 5

## Special Rules
- **Reactive Growth**: Strength is not reduced by wounds; instead, strength *increases* by one for each wound the Queemer incurs.
