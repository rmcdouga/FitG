# Overview
Religious sect that exerts powerful control over much of the local populace.

## Combat Stats
- **Strength**: 5
- **Endurance**: 4

## Special Rules
- **Political Impact**: If defeated by *Rebel* characters (endurance reaches "0"), the planet's Loyalty Marker is shifted one space in the Rebels' favor (may trigger Domino effect).
- **Imperial Interaction**: Fight Imperial characters as well, but no shift occurs if they win.
