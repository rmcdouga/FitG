# Overview
Carnivorous plant that grabs one character (drawn at random by opposing player). Other characters in Mission Group battle the vegetation.

## Combat Stats
- **Attack Strength**: 0
- **Endurance**: 5

## Special Rules
- **Entwine**: After each round of combat that the plant survives, the entwined character incurs one wound. If no other characters are on the mission, the grabbed character is consumed.
