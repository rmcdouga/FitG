# Overview
Vicious wolf-like creatures that hunt their prey in packs.

## Combat Stats
- **Strength**: 5
- **Endurance**: 4

## Special Rules
- **Pack Mentality**: If Gadhars survive two rounds of combat, more show up. Add *two* to the current strength and endurance of attacking Gadhars every odd-numbered combat round beginning with the third.
