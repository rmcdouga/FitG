# Overview
Squad of flying insects frightened by characters, whom they proceed to sting.

## Combat Stats
- **Attack**: Each character is attacked once on the "0" differential column of the Character Combat Results Table only.

## Special Rules
- **Lifecycle**: Insects die after stinging once.
- **Break-off**: No break-off possible.
