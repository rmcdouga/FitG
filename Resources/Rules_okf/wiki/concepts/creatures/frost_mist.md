# Overview
Intelligent spirit that wraps itself around its victims and freezes them to death.

## Combat Stats
- **Strength**: 5
- **Endurance**: 5

## Special Rules
- **Frost Aura**: Attacks are not rolled for; after each round of combat, each character incurs one wound (unless the creature was killed in that round).
- **Break-off**: No break-off possible.
