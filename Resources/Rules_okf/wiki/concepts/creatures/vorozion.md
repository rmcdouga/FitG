# Overview
Highly evolved, hostile thought being; very impatient.

## Combat Stats
- **Intelligence**: 6 (matched against combined Intelligence ratings of characters).

## Special Rules
- **Mental Assault**: Characters may not fight back or break-off.
- **Patience**: Vorozion continues to attack only until a round occurs in which it fails to inflict any wounds, at which point it leaves in disgust.
