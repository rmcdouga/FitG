# Overview
Electric intelligence which ensnares mission group.

## Combat Stats
- **Attack**: Each character attacked individually on the "0" column of the Character Combat Results Table each round.

## Special Rules
- **Defense**: Characters cannot fight back.
- **Victory**: Combat continues until the character succeeds in breaking off (resolved on "0" column).
