# Overview
Savage wooly mammoth that exudes slow poisonous gas.

## Combat Stats
- **Base Strength**: 5
- **Endurance**: 4

## Special Rules
- **Stamina**: Loss of endurance does not affect strength; instead, it gains a strength point with each round of combat starting with the second.
