# Overview
Friendly, furry creature that does not attack.

## Interaction
- **Imperial**: Ignores all Imperial characters (no effect).
- **Rebel**: Senses good vibrations and gives them an ancient family heirloom.

## Reward
The Rebel player draws one Possession Card. If a spaceship is drawn, it is returned to the deck and another card is drawn (Zop's cave is too small for spaceships).

## Limitation
The Zop may only be encountered by the Rebels once.
