# Overview
An unheard-of cross-breed between a lion-like creature and a reptile, incredibly ferocious and stealthy.

## Combat Stats
- **Strength**: 5
- **Endurance**: 4

## Special Rules
- **Stealth**: Receives a surprise shift of one column to the right on the first round; no break-off allowed on the first round.
