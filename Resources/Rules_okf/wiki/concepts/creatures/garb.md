# Overview
Two-headed feline creature with two conflicting personalities.

## Combat Stats
- **Strength**: 7
- **Endurance**: 6

## Special Rules
- **Self-Conflict**: After each round of combat, the creature attacks itself. Roll the die and refer to the attacker's side of the "+ 1" column of the Character Combat Results Table and apply wounds.
