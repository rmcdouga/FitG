# Overview
Ordinary-looking rock formation that is actually sentient and quite poisonous.

## Effect
Unwary characters sit on it until they realize their discomfort.
**Damage**: Subtract the single highest Intelligence rating in the group from 4. The resulting difference is the number of Wounds that *each* character receives.
