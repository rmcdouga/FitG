# Overview
Telepathic being that battles characters with conjured images of cutthroats and savages.

## Combat Stats
- **Strength**: 9
- **Endurance**: 4

## Special Rules
- **Counter-Intelligence**: Any one character's Intelligence rating may be subtracted from the creature's strength rating each round (including the first).
- **Break-off**: One is added to all break-off rolls.
