# Overview
Clan of streetwise thieves.

## Combat Stats
- **Strength**: 5
- **Endurance**: 4

## Special Rules
- **Theft**: If both sides survive following three rounds of combat, the thieves run away with any one of the Mission Group's non-spaceship possessions (picked at random).
- **Duration**: No break-off; combat ends after three rounds no matter what.
