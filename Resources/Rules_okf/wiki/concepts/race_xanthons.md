# Overview
The Xanthons possess incredible strength and viciousness, but only when in heat. In temperate areas they are docile; in cold areas they cannot survive.

## Distribution
- **Home Planet**: Xan (513)
- **Colonies**: Mitrith (143), Capilax (522), and Scythia (531).

## Traits
Technologically behind other star-faring races and largely uninvolved in galactic politics.
