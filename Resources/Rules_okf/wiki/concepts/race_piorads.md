# Overview
The Piorads are structured into a hierarchy of tribes. They are characterized by their immense wealth and a tendency to hoard it, yielding only the minimum necessary to avoid Imperial retaliation.

## Distribution
- **Home Planet**: Ayod (451)
- **Colonies**: Ownex (141), Lysenda (232), Solvia (432), Cercis (433), and Tartio (442).

## Traits
They are among the galaxy's best spacecraft handlers and fighters.
