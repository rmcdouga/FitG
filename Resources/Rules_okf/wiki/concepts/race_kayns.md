# Overview
The Kayns are known for their absolute loyalty to whoever or whatever they have been brought up to serve. Their society is heavily militarized and they provide the Empire with many of its best soldiers.

## Distribution
- **Home Planet**: Mimulus (I 1 1)
- **Colonies**: Kalgar (121), Etreg (212), and Niconi (551).

## Traits
Beyond Imperial service, some Kayns operate as free-lance mercenaries or bodyguards.
