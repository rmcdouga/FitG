# Overview
Ascaill is a Rhone dictator who exerts tyrannical control over the industrial megalopolis of Pronox.

## Role in the Empire
Backed by powerful Imperial support, Ascaill squeezes profitable production rates out of his subjects to provide the Empire with weapons and communication devices.
