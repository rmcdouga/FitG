# Overview
An Imperial loyalist and king of the feline-like race on Heliax.

## Military Prowess
The pride of King Odel's army has been a significant asset to the Empire's greatest campaigns. His son, **Odene Hobar**, fled Heliax in disgust over the Empire's methods of conquest.
