# Overview
A former commander of the most feared fleet of Piorad raiders.

## Rise to Power
After being destroyed by the Empire, Nam Nhuk returned to Ownex under a new guise and wrested control from a corrupt Imperial puppet. He is now the leader of the resident Piorads.
