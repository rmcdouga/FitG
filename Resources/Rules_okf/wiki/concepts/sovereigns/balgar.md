# Overview
Balgar rose to power in the same way as other politicians on Mrane but distinguished himself through scientific development.

## Achievements
He saved his home world from natural disaster by preventing the freezing of habitable water when their sun, Luine, cooled unexpectedly and temporarily. He led a fleet of Mrane's militia to re-ignite the combustion rate on Luine.
