# Overview
The Galactic Game is the most complex level of play, utilizing the full game-map.

## Scope
- Rules sections 29.0 through 38.0.
- Introduces Imperial and Rebel resources, taxation, and strategic assignments.
- All rules from Star System and Province games apply.
