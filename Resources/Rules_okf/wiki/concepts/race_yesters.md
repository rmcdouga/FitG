# Overview
The Yesters are a curious, bird-like race that thrives in the clouds and wind currents of hydrogen-rich atmospheres.

## Distribution
- **Home Planet**: Cieson (211)
- **Colonies**: Magro (112), Angoff (222), Charkhan (223), Pronox (231), and Tamset (312).

## Traits
Known for their high intuitive intelligence and unique stellar-sail spacecraft.
