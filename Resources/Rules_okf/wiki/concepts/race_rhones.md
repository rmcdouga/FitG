# Overview
The Rhones are the founders of the Empire. Their origin is unknown, though they have colonized extensively across the galaxy.

## Distribution
Found on numerous planets including Margro (112), Adare (142), Jura (151), Thomas (152), Liomax (161), Orlog (162), Pronox (231), Orning (241), Chim (311), Tsipa (323), Akubera (342), Troliso (411), Lonica (421), Adrax (523), and Trov (542).
