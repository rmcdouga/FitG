# Overview
The game is played in repeating sequences called **Game-Turns**, where one month of real time passes.

## Structure
Each Game-Turn consists of:
1. **Rebel Player-Turn**
2. **Imperial Player-Turn**
3. **Game-Turn Interphase** (checking for Rebel-controlled planets)

## Player-Turn Phases
Every player-turn is divided into three phases:
- **Operations Phase**: Movement and Military Combat.
- **Search Phase**: Attempting to find detected characters.
- **Mission Phase**: Assigning and resolving character missions.
