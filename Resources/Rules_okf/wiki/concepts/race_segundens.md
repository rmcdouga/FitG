# Overview
The Segundens are distinguished by their extreme intelligence and focus on technological development rather than expansion or conquest. They maintain a peaceful submission to the Empire based on calculated resource/life costs of open war.

## Distribution
- **Home Planet**: Bajukai (122)
- **Colonies**: Tiglyf (131), Lysenda (232), Lonica (421), and Aras (521).

## Traits
Their technology is far beyond that of most other planets in the galaxy.
