# Overview
The Suvans live in pavilions just beneath the water's surface and are skilled at extracting resources from their liquid habitat.

## Distribution
- **Home Planet**: Mrane (351)
- **Colonies**: Fliad (113), Orning (241), Midest (341), and Akubera (342).

## Traits
While adaptable to breathing outside of water, they are physically much weaker when not in their natural habitat.
