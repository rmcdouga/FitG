# Overview
A PDB is a planet-wide system of equipment used by the controlling player to detect and attack enemy forces.

## Levels of Effectiveness
PDBs operate at one of three levels (0, 1, or 2). Higher levels are more effective at hindering enemies.

## Status
- **Up**: Operating normally.
- **Down**: Unable to function.

## Interaction
The current level is indicated by the PDB marker's position on the Political Track.
