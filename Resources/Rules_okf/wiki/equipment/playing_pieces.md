# The Playing Pieces

The game uses various physical components to represent presence and status:

## Types of Pieces
- **Character Counters:** Used to track the movement and location of characters.
- **Military Units:** Represent substantial forces on the map.
- **Markers:** Used for tracking political status, such as PDB Up/Down or Loyalty levels.
