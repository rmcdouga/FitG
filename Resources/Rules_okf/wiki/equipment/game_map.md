# The Game-Map

The 22\" x 34\" game-map portrays a \"slice\" of the central portion of the galactic empire.

## Map Features
The various circles and tracks on the map represent:
- **Star Systems:** Detailed political, economic, racial and geographic information for each important planet.
- **Game-Turn Record Track:** Tracks the passage of time through game-turns.
- **Planet Tracks:** Individual tracks used to record loyalty and other status updates.
