# The Cards

The game utilizes several distinct types of cards:

## Card Types
- **Character Cards:** (32 total) Describe the attributes and special talents of key personages.
- **Possession Cards:** (20 total) Represent spaceships, robots, and other objects of aid.
- **Mission Cards:** (15 total) Outline specific objectives for players to achieve.
- **Action Cards:** (30 total) Used during the Mission Phase to resolve events.
- **Galactic Event Cards:** (29 total) Used in the Galactic Game to trigger large-scale changes.
- **Strategic Assignment Cards:** (14 total) Govern Imperial movement during the Galactic Game.
