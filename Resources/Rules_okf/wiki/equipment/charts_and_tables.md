# Charts and Tables

The following reference tools are essential for resolving various game functions:

## Key Reference Aids
- **Detection Table:** For calculating success during the Detection Routine.
- **Military Combat Results Table:** To resolve combat between military units.
- **Search Table:** Used during the Search Phase of a mission.
- **Character Combat Results Table:** To resolve character-on-character engagements.
- **Force Point Cost Chart:** Details the cost to purchase units and upgrade PDBs.
- **Hyperjump Table:** Determines results for interstellar travel.
- **Planet Control Summary:** A quick look at empire stability.
- **Character Endurance Sheets:** To track survival and wounds.
