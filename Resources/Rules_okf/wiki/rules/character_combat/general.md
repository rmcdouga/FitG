# Overview
Character combat involves a struggle between an attacking force and a defending force.

## Forces
- **Attacking Force**: The characters, locals, creatures, or military squad initiating the attack.
- **Defending Force**: The characters being attacked.

## Key Mechanics
- **Active vs Non-active**: Defenders may be in one of these two states during combat.
- **Break-Off**: An attempt by defending characters to escape unscathed from the attackers.
- **Endurance**: A measure of stamina; characters receive wounds until their endurance reaches zero, at which point they die.

## Resolution
Combat is resolved using the Character Combat Results Table (12.9).
