# Overview
The struggle over planets involves moving the Loyalty Marker to change a planet's political status.

## Core Mechanics
- **Loyalty Marker**: Moves along the Political Track (from Imperial/Patriotic toward Rebel/Unrest).
- **Starting a Rebellion**: Triggered when specific conditions are met (see Case 15.2).
- **Stopping a Rebellion**: Opposing forces attempt to stabilize the planet (see Case 15.3).

## Planet Control
Control of a planet determines who can use the Planetary Defense Base (PDB). If a planet is controlled by neither player, the PDB is unusable.
