# Overview
Possessions (spaceships, robots, weapons, etc.) provide aid to Rebel characters.

## Acquisition and Assignment
- Possessions are assigned to characters who can use them.
- Possession cards are kept hidden under character cards so opposing players cannot see them until declared.

## Use and Loss
Items may be lost or become inoperative during play. Reference Section 27.0 for repair rules.

## Categories
- **Companions**: Specialized allies.
- **Weapons and Objects**: Equipment used to aid characters.
- **Spaceships**: Crucial for interplanetary travel (Rebel vs Imperial).
