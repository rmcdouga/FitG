# Overview
Hyperjumping allows characters and units to move over great distances in deep space.

## Mechanics
1. **Distance**: Determined by the Hyperjump Distance rules (21.1).
2. **Navigation**: Based on the same's Navigation Rating (21.2).
3. **Resolution**: Use the Hyperjump Table (21.3) to determine success/failure.

## Risks
- **Drift**: An off-course hyperjump can result in "Drift" or "Drift 2" (21.4, 21.5).
- **Detection**: Hyperjumping may trigger detection by Enemy PDBs (21.6).
