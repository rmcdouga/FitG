# Overview
In the Province Game, players use Force Points to expand their military capabilities and maintain planetary defense.

## Force Point Usage
Force Points are spent on:
- **Purchasing Military Units**: Buying new units based on the Force Point Cost Chart (19.7).
- **Planetary Defense Bases (PDB)**: Expending a Force Point can put a "Down" PDB back to "Up" status.

## Unit Restrictions
Certain restrictions apply to the purchase of Imperial military units to ensure game balance and tactical variety.
