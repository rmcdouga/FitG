# Overview
Missions are attempts by characters to achieve objectives beneficial to the owning player.

## Process
1. **Assignment**: In an Environ, a Player assigns Mission Cards to character groups (Mission Groups).
2. **Action**: Resolve missions by drawing Action Cards. This incorporates "Action Events" and "Mission Letters."
3. **Completion**: If the correct Mission Letter is drawn, the mission is complete.

## Bonus Draws
Characters who survive Action Events but don't complete their mission may receive bonus draws based on their attributes and the own Mission Card requirements.
