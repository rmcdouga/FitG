# Overview
Military combat occurs between opposing military units in the same Environ.

## Initiation
Either player may declare military combat. The initiating player is the **attacker**, and the other is the **defender**.

## Leadership
Each player may name one character present in the Environ as the leader of their military units.

## Resolution
Combat is resolved using the Military Combat Results Table (10.8).
