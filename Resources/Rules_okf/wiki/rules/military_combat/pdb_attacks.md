# Overview
A Player controlling a planet may use its PDB to attack Enemy military units entering or leaving the planet.

## Attack Resolution
Attacks are resolved on the Military Combat Results Table (10.8) based on PDB level:
- **Level 2 PDB**: Resolved on the 3-1 column.
- **Level 1 PDB**: Resolved on the 1-1 column.
- **Level 0 PDB**: Cannot be used to attack military units.

## PDB Damage
If a PDB incurs losses during an attack:
- **Loss of 1**: No effect.
- **Loss of 2**: PDB is placed **Down**.
- **Loss of 3+**: PDB is placed **Down** and reduced by one level (e.g., Level 2 $\rightarrow$ Level 1).

## Restrictions
Military units may never attack an Enemy PDB; they may only defend against its attacks.
