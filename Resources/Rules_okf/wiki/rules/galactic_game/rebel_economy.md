# Overview
The Rebels operate from a hidden base while managing their own limited resources.

## The Rebel Secret Base
- **Selection**: A secret base is chosen at the start of play (34.1).
- **Reveal**: The base may be revealed to the Imperial player under certain conditions (34.2).

## Resource Track
The Rebel Resource Track manages the ability to purchase military units (34.3, 34.4).
