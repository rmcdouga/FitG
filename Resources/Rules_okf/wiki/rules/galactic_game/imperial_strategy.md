# Overview
The Empire uses strategic assignments and atrocities to maintain control over the galaxy.

## Strategic Assignments
Ordered via the Strategic Assignment Deck (35.1), these regulate Imperial movement and goals.

## Imperial Atrocities
Imperial Atrocity Units can be committed under specific conditions (36.2) to exert pressure or punish populations.
