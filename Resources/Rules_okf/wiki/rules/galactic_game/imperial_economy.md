# Overview
The Imperial player manages a resource track to fund their military machine.

## Taxation
Taxing provinces provides the resources necessary for the Empire's operation (33.1).

## Spending and Maintenance
Imperial Force Points are spent on maintenance and unit acquisition, as tracked on the Imperial Resource Track (33.2, 33.3).
